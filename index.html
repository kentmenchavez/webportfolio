ABOUT ME

My name is Kent Menchavez, and I go by the nickname Keni. I graduated in 2017 from the University of San Jose - Recoletos with a bachelor's degree in Management Accounting. In the early stages of my career, I worked as an accounting staff, handling responsibilities such as financial reporting, payroll, bank reconciliation, inventory management database, and filing tax returns.

Due to the pandemic, I had to seek a job in an industry that was stable and essential during those challenging times. Fortunately, I landed a position at Converge ICT Solutions, Inc. as an Order Management Associate. I was part of the pioneer team when the company began operations in the VisMin region. My primary role focused on end-to-end order fulfillment, where we validated system data and documents for all applications across various sales channels. I contributed significantly to some order process improvements and automated certain productivity monitoring tasks. Since 2020, I have been immensely grateful for the opportunities this company has provided.

Now, I aspire to pursue my interest in programming and become a software developer. I aim to create system tools that can positively impact businesses, particularly in the telco industry. I am passionate about data and eager to make a difference in how businesses utilize data to make informed decisions.
